FAQs

What exactly is a shipping agent, and how can your company help?

In simple terms, it is a shipping agent who with a local expert acts as a representative of the owner of the ship and carries out all essential duties and obligations required by the crew of the ship.

At New Visualization Ltd, we provide shipping agency services to our clients who require assistance with their shipping operations. Our team of experienced professionals can handle all aspects of the shipping process, from arranging for the transportation of goods to managing the necessary documentation.

What types of maritime services do you offer?

we offer a wide range of maritime services to help our clients navigate the complex world of shipping and logistics. Some of the maritime services we offer include:

Ship brokering: We handle the buying and selling of ships, as well as the chartering and leasing of vessels.

Port agency services: We can handle all aspects of a ship's port call, including coordinating with port authorities, arranging for cargo and fuel deliveries, and handling any necessary paperwork.

Cargo handling: We provide comprehensive cargo handling services, including loading and unloading, warehousing, and transportation.

Marine surveying: We offer marine surveying services to help ensure the safety and compliance of vessels, equipment, and cargo.

Maritime logistics: We provide end-to-end logistics solutions for all types of maritime operations, from port calls to offshore shipments.

Our goal is to provide our clients with comprehensive and flexible maritime services that meet their unique needs and requirements.

What is your process for booking air travel or making hotel reservations?

we have a dedicated team that handles air ticketing and hotel reservation services. Here is a breakdown of our process:

For air ticketing:
1. Understanding your travel requirements: We'll work with you to understand your travel dates, preferred airlines, budget, and any other special requests you may have.
2. Research and options generation: Based on your requirements, we'll research and generate options for you, including flight schedules, routes, prices, and other details.
3. Booking and confirmation: Once you have selected your preferred flight, we'll handle the booking process for you and provide you with the confirmation.

For hotel reservations:
1. Understanding your accommodation requirements: We'll work with you to understand your preferred location, budget, travel dates, and any other special requests you may have.
2. Research and options generation: Based on your requirements, we'll research and generate options for you, including hotels, resorts, or accommodations that meet your criteria.
3. Booking and confirmation: Once you have selected your preferred accommodation, we'll handle the booking process for you and provide you with the confirmation.

Throughout the process, our team will communicate with you regularly to ensure your satisfaction and to address any questions or concerns you may have. Our goal is to provide you with a seamless and hassle-free experience when it comes to air ticketing and hotel reservations.
